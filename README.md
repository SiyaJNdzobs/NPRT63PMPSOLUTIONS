# E-RANK: Digital Taxi Rank Management

## Project Overview

E-RANK is an innovative digital taxi rank management system designed to transform the way taxi ranks operate by improving efficiency, safety, and communication for all users — from passengers and drivers to marshals and taxi owners. By digitizing queue management, passenger registration, and fare recording, E-RANK aims to modernize the traditional minibus taxi industry while respecting existing workflows.

## Mission

To empower taxi marshals and streamline taxi rank operations through accessible digital tools, enhancing passenger safety and operational transparency.

## Vision

To become the leading digital platform that revolutionizes taxi rank management, fostering safer and more efficient public transportation networks.

## Benefits to Users

- **Taxi Marshals:** Simplified queue control and passenger data management  
- **Drivers:** Clear, dispute-free queue system with real-time updates  
- **Taxi Owners:** Transparent trip monitoring and operational insights  
- **Passengers:** Improved safety, better communication, and reliable travel information  

## Why E-RANK is Feasible

E-RANK leverages affordable mobile and tablet technology already familiar to users, minimizing barriers to adoption. The system respects existing marshal workflows, digitizing rather than replacing critical manual processes. This balance ensures practical implementation in the South African taxi industry context, offering immediate value while encouraging gradual digital transformation.
